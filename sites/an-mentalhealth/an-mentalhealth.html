Mental Health Disparities within the Aleut Region

Case Study by Mark Blinn & Jennifer Huff

The Aleut Region in Alaska is home to the Aleut. There are mental health disparities that have become a contemporary part of their culture. In looking broadly at the issues, it can be seen that PTSD is part of the explanatory model that then sheds light on such issues as alchoholism in the older generations and now includes substance abuse within the younger generations.
